Reusable capability (invoked by a daily brief task, or on demand). Use `WebSearch` (load via ToolSearch) for fresh sources; **cite links + dates**; keep reporting neutral and factual; concise digest (~2–4 bullets per section), not essays. Skip a section if nothing notable. This is an internal briefing — it informs the team, it is never published externally.

## Suggested sections (customize for your domain)
- **Industry news** — notable developments in your sector (cite source + date).
- **Research and tools** — new papers, releases, or repos relevant to your work (e.g., agent frameworks, ML tooling, platform engineering).
- **Regulatory / policy** — any relevant policy or compliance developments.
- **Trending** — themes circulating in relevant communities (summarize neutrally; don't reproduce harmful content).

Output a `## News & signal` block for the morning brief.
